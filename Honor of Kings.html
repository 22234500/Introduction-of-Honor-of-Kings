Honor of Kings

1. Game Introduction

1.1 Background

'Honor of Kings' is a MOBA domestic mobile game operated by Tecent Game TiMi studio group. The game can be downloaded on Android, IOS and NS platform. The European and American versions of Honor of Kings are Arena Of Valor.

1.2 Game Mode

This is a competitive battle game, it is mainly dominate the 5v5 team game mode to push off the opponent's base first to win.

1.3 Characters Setting

The positions of characters can be divided into mages, warriors, tanks, assassins, shooters, support, and different heroes have different attributes and skills. Players buy equipment in matches to improve their combat ability and survivability, each person can only purchase up to 6 pieces of weaponry, and some characters have exclusive equipment.

3.Featured system

3.1 Social system

  • Buddy system

    • After adding friends, players can increase intimacy by playing in teams and sending coins to each other. It is also possible to build intimacy relationship and social circle.

  • Clan system

    • Players can create their own clan or join someone else's, and then compete in a clan challenge with their teammates

  • Mentoring system

    • Players in the game can establish a mentor-apprentice relationship, the master needs to be one rank higher than the apprentice. After completing missions, players can get free skin.
